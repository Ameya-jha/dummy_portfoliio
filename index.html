A bit about me.

I'm Jake — I design and build the back-end of online communities. Most of my time goes into Discord: custom bots, moderation tooling, and the kind of server structure that keeps things running smoothly once a community starts to grow.

Over the years I've moderated and developed for servers ranging from small startups to communities of 30,000+ members, which means I've seen what breaks at scale — and I build with that in mind from day one.
